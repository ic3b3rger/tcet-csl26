Cybersecurity and Laws

CSL26 | Semester - Year

Understanding the intersection of technology, security, and legal frameworks

About the Course

Course Description

This course integrates technical cybersecurity skills with legal knowledge and ethical reasoning. Students learn network security fundamentals through hands-on labs using tools like Wireshark and Kali Linux, covering packet analysis, common attack techniques, threat modeling, and digital forensics. Simultaneously, students study Indian cyber law (IT Act 2000/2008, DPDP Act 2023, IT Rules 2021), data protection frameworks, electronic evidence requirements, and global regulatory approaches.
